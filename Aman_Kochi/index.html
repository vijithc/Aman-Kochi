Medical Tourism

Medical tourism can be broadly defined as provision of ‘cost effective’ medical care in collaboration with the tourism industry for patients needing surgical and other forms of specialized treatment. This process is being facilitated by the corporate sector involved in medical care as well as the tourism industry - both private and public.
